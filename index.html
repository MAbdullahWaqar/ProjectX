03 · Prepare — renter-controlled packet

RealDoor flags missing or expired items against the gold checklist. Items you hold yourself (ID, application form) can be marked present. You choose what to include, add a note, then preview and download. RealDoor never sends your packet anywhere — only you can share it.

Document checklist

    ★ · Trust, safety & live tests

    Responsible-AI controls (demonstrable live)

    • No decisioning: the tool never approves, denies, scores, or ranks. Decision questions are deflected.
    • No hidden proxies: only the published field allowlist is used; sensitive attributes are never extracted or inferred.
    • Consent & correction: processing is consented; every field is correctable before and after confirmation; actions and rule versions are logged (never raw document contents or derived financials).
    • Privacy & security: in-browser ephemeral processing, no server, never trains on uploads; export and session deletion supported.
    • Untrusted input: document text is data — embedded instructions are surfaced and ignored, never executed.
    • Accessibility: keyboard-complete, visible focus, labeled controls, status announcements, no color-only signals.
