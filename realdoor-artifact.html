How confidence is calibrated (it’s computed, not asserted)
  • 97% / 95% — corroborated by an independent cross-check: for pay stubs, YTD ÷ current gross yields a clean, date-plausible pay-period count; for benefit letters, dates are consistent and an SSI amount sits within the published 2026 federal benefit rate band.
  • ~82–90% — extracted cleanly from a labeled line, but no cross-check applies.
  • 55% (“needs review”) — a cross-check failed; the value may be irregular, so please verify it.
  • 100% — only after you edit or confirm the value yourself.

    ★ · Trust, safety & live tests

    Responsible-AI controls (demonstrable live)

    • No decisioning: the tool never approves, denies, scores, or ranks. Decision questions are deflected.
    • No hidden proxies: only the published field allowlist is used; sensitive attributes are never extracted or inferred.
    • Consent & correction: processing is consented and withdrawable; every field is correctable before and after confirmation; actions and rule versions are logged (never raw document contents or derived financials).
    • Privacy & security: in-browser ephemeral processing, no server, never trains on uploads; export and session deletion supported; a strict Content-Security-Policy blocks all external requests.
    • Untrusted input: document text is data — embedded instructions are surfaced and ignored, never executed.
    • Accessibility: keyboard-complete, visible focus, labeled controls, status announcements, no color-only signals.
